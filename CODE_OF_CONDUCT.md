# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want taking part in Prompt Refine — through issues, pull requests, and discussions — to
be a friendly and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or
experience level.

## Our standards

- Be considerate, welcoming, and constructive.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully, and assume good intent.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

Disruptive conduct — such as personal attacks, harassment, or sustained bad-faith
behavior — is not acceptable in any project space.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces (the repository, issues, pull requests, and
discussions) and when an individual is representing the project in public.

## Baseline and enforcement

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org)
(version 2.1) as its baseline standard. To report a concern, contact the maintainer
[@Li-Bailiang](https://github.com/Li-Bailiang) privately — for example through a GitHub
private security advisory or a direct message. Reports are reviewed and handled
confidentially, and the maintainer will respond as promptly as is reasonable.
